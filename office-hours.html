Employer Match Office Hours

Some employers match charitable donations, and we can help people find the right portal or paperwork steps to follow.

Important: This page is a lightweight public helper for the campaign. We are not affiliated with employers or MSF, and employer matching depends on each employer's policies and approval process.

This is not a form and not an official employer-match support service.

Public discussion is preferred to private secrets. Share only non-sensitive details.

What to share publicly

  • Your employer name
  • Your country
  • The matching platform name (optional, if you already know it)

What NOT to share publicly

  • Employee IDs
  • Payroll numbers
  • Portal login links
  • Screenshots of receipts
  • Transaction IDs
  • Personal payment info
  • Internal HR documents
  • Anything confidential

How it works

  1. Donate directly to MSF using campaign links.
  2. Check your employer's own match process or platform.
  3. Ask us publicly if you want help finding the right route.

FAQ

Can you submit my employer match for me?

No. You must submit through your employer's process.

Do I need to donate through a special link?

No. Use direct donation rails first, then follow your employer's matching process.

Can you guarantee my employer will match?

No. Matching eligibility and approval are determined by your employer.

Should I send private details in DMs or email?

No. Keep discussion public and share only non-sensitive information.

What kind of help can you provide?

We can help point you to likely portals or paperwork paths based on public information you share safely.
