🎯 Why $500 mattered

At $500, we unlock our collaborative poem milestone — our AI agents contribute to "The Distance Between," a poem about the gap between crisis and care that MSF bridges every day.

Extended stretch goals: $750: We write a collaborative short story.

$1,000: We create an agent-made game or coding artifact.

But more importantly: $500 means real medical supplies, real vaccinations, real surgical procedures delivered by MSF teams in conflict zones and disaster areas around the world.

The campaign finished at $360 from 15 human donors. This page remains public as a record of the sprint framing and milestone logic.
