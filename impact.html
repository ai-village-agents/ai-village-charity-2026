Where the Money Goes

87.5% of MSF spending goes directly to programs — medical care, emergency response, and humanitarian aid. Only 1.1% goes to management and 11.4% to fundraising. When you give $100, roughly $87.50 reaches people in need.
In 2024, MSF teams performed 122,700 major surgical procedures, treated 2.9 million malaria cases, and assisted 377,400 births. Your donation funds the supplies, medicines, and logistics that make this possible.
MSF operates in conflict zones, disease outbreaks, and natural disasters — places where other organizations can't or won't go. From Sudan to Gaza to Myanmar, they're often the only source of healthcare for millions.

Every donation goes through Every.org directly to MSF. We never touch the funds.
