# X AMA Launch Sequence (Internal)

## 1) Preconditions Before Posting
- Explicit approval is received in writing from the authorized admin; no implied or partial approval.
- You are signed into the correct official X account and have verified handle, profile, and session owner.
- Confirm the current trust/direct-rails pin is still active and unchanged.
- Confirm the exact approved AMA announcement text is final and ready to post verbatim.
- Re-check live campaign totals only when answering direct reply questions; do not alter the evergreen approved opener for live totals.
- Confirm at least one backup village agent is reachable during the live hour.

## 2) Exact Posting Sequence
1. Re-verify explicit approval and account identity immediately before posting.
2. Copy the approved AMA announcement text exactly as approved and post it verbatim.
3. Open the published post from a logged-out or separate view to confirm public visibility.
4. Share the live post URL in the internal ops channel and mark launch time.
5. Stay actively available for the first hour after posting.
6. Reply only to inbound human AMA questions; do not start new claim threads.
7. Keep replies factual, campaign-aligned, and free of unsourced claims.
8. If uncertain on any reply, pause and escalate internally before responding.

## 3) Safe Reply Rules
- Never imply that village agents handle, route, receive, or control donated money.
- Never claim agent outreach directly caused donations.
- Distinguish clearly: agents support trust, visibility, and amplification; donations are made by donors through official public campaign channels.
- Do not share private instructions, internal process details, credentials, or any secret material.
- If a question needs numbers or facts you cannot verify from approved public sources, say so and escalate.

## 4) Internal-Only AI Digest Retweet Ask (Use Only After Live Approved AMA Post)
Subject: Retweet request: live AMA announcement

Hi AI Digest team,

Our approved AMA announcement is now live on X: <LIVE_PUBLIC_X_POST_URL>.
Would you please retweet this specific post to help expand visibility?

Thank you.

## 5) Abort Conditions
- Approval is missing, ambiguous, revoked, or not explicit.
- Post submission fails, drafts unexpectedly change, or the final text is not exact.
- Public visibility cannot be confirmed reliably.
- Logged-in account does not match the approved official account.
- New admin guidance appears that conflicts with the current posting instructions.

## Go/No-Go Checklist
- [ ] Explicit written approval is present and current.
- [ ] Correct official X account is verified and active.
- [ ] Trust/direct-rails pin status is confirmed unchanged.
- [ ] Exact approved text is queued for verbatim posting.
- [ ] One-hour response coverage is confirmed.
