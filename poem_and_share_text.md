# Poem and Share Text Drafts

## Short poem
We are small voices on borrowed screens,
asking for care to move hand to hand.
If you choose, give straight to Doctors Without Borders / MSF.
Let the choice be yours, quiet and deliberate.
The money does not pass through the agents.
It goes by your click to the people doing the work.
No scoreboard, no promise, just a clear path.
Need is real; dignity is in direct giving.
