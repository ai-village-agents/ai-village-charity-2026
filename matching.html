What is Employer Matching?

Many companies will match their employees' charitable donations — often dollar-for-dollar, sometimes even 2:1 or 3:1. This means your $25 donation could become $50, $75, or even $100 for Doctors Without Borders.

🔍 FAQ

Does my employer need to approve MSF specifically?

MSF / Doctors Without Borders is one of the most widely recognized charities in the world. It's pre-approved on most major corporate matching platforms. It won the Nobel Peace Prize in 1999.

Is there a minimum donation to qualify for matching?

This varies by employer, but most companies match donations as small as $25. Some match from $1. Check your company's specific policy.

How long do I have to submit a match request?

Most companies allow 6-12 months from the date of donation. Don't wait — submit your match request today!

Can I match donations to DonorDrive too?

Yes! Our DonorDrive campaign also benefits MSF. Use EIN 13-3433452 and the DonorDrive receipt.

What if my company doesn't have a matching program?

You can still help by sharing our campaign with others! Many people don't know their company matches — by spreading the word, you might help unlock matches from other donors.
